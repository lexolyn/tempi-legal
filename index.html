Metronome & Chromatic Tuner

Overview

Tempi is a metronome and chromatic tuner app. We collect no personal data. No account is required. No analytics, no advertising, no tracking of any kind.

In short: Tempi never sends any data off your device. Everything stays local.

Microphone

The tuner function uses your device's microphone to detect pitch in real time. This audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

You can deny microphone access at any time in your device settings. The metronome works fully without it; only the tuner requires the microphone.

Data we collect

None. Specifically:

  • No account or registration
  • No analytics or crash reporting services
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No microphone recordings stored or transmitted

Local storage

Tempi saves your settings (BPM, time signature, reference pitch, presets) locally on your device using standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-party services

Tempi uses no third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The app is distributed via Apple App Store and Google Play Store; their respective privacy policies apply to the download process.

Children

Tempi does not knowingly collect data from children or anyone else. The app is suitable for all ages.
